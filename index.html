About Wildlife Guardians

We are a worldwide coalition committed to animal welfare and biodiversity preservation.

Our Mission

Wildlife Guardians aims to secure a vibrant future for animals by combating poaching, restoring habitats, and raising awareness for ethical treatment.

Launched in 2008, we've evolved from a regional effort to a global force with more than 60,000 supporters in 130 countries.

Our methods blend field operations, educational programs, and international lobbying to confront the foremost threats to wildlife.
